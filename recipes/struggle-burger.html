Struggle Burger

About this meal

This burger is good for rough times,
when there aren't many ingredients available.

Ingredients

  • Two burger buns
  • Pack of instant noodles
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Put two buns on the table
  2. Get the toppings on the buns
  3. Prepare instant noodles and put them on the buns
  4. Close the burger and eat
