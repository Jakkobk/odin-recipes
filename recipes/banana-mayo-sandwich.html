Banana Mayo Sandwich

About this meal

This sandwich is good for rough times,
when there aren't many ingredients available.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Enough mayonnaise to cover both slices
  • A banana

Steps

  1. Put two slices of bread on the table
  2. Get the mayonnaise on the bread
  3. Now cut the banana into slices and put the slices on the bread
  4. Close the bread and eat
