Hot dog

About this meal

This hot dog is good for rough times,
when there aren't many ingredients available.

Ingredients

  • One slice of bread
  • A sausage
  • Ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Put one slice of bread on the table
  2. Put the sausage on the bread
  3. Get the ketchup and mustard on the sausage
  4. Enjoy!
